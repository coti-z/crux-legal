# Scanio Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2026

---

Scanio ("we", "us", "our") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Scanio application ("App").

By using the App, you consent to the information practices described in this Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Camera Data

- The App accesses your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes and QR codes.
- Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

### 1.2 Scan History

- Scanned and generated codes (value, type, symbology, date) are stored locally on your device.
- This data is not uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

### 1.3 Advertising Data

- The free version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.
- Google AdMob may automatically collect device identifiers, IP address, usage data, and other information as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- Subscribing to the premium plan removes all advertisements and associated third-party data collection.

### 1.4 Subscription Data

- Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store.
- We do not collect, process, or store any payment or financial information.

### 2.1 Service Provision

- Providing barcode and QR code scanning and generation features

- Displaying advertisements to users on the free plan via Google AdMob

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- All user data (scan history, saved codes) is stored locally on your device.
- We do not operate any servers that collect or store your personal data.

### 4.2 Security

- On-device data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).
- We do not have access to any data stored on your device.

## 6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

- Deleting individual items from the scan history within the App
- Uninstalling the App from your device

No request to us is necessary as we do not hold any of your data on external servers.
