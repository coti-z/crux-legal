## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I scan a QR code or barcode?
Open the app and go to the **Scan** tab. Point your camera at the code and it will be detected automatically.

### How do I create a QR code?
Go to the **Create** tab. Select the type of QR code you want (URL, WiFi, Contact, Email, etc.), enter the information, and your code will be generated instantly.

### How do I restore my subscription?
Go to **Settings > Restore Purchases**. If you previously purchased Scanio Pro, your subscription will be restored.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to **iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Scanio** to manage or cancel your subscription.

### Where is my scan history?
All scanned and created codes are saved in the **History** tab. You can search and filter your past scans.

### Is my data stored on a server?
No. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data on external servers.

### How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual items from the History tab, or uninstall the app to remove all data.
