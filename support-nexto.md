## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create a project?
Tap the **+** button and select **New Project**. Enter your project name, set a deadline, and start adding tasks.

### How do I add tasks to a project?
Open a project and tap the **+** button to create a new task. You can set due dates and track completion.

### How do I view my calendar?
Go to the **Calendar** tab to see your projects and tasks laid out on a calendar grid.

### How do I check my productivity stats?
Go to the **Statistics** section to view your project completion rates, streaks, and average progress.

### Is my data synced across devices?
Nexto supports real-time data synchronization. Check the sync status indicator to confirm your data is up to date.

### How do I use the home screen widget?
Long press on your home screen, tap **+**, search for **Nexto**, and add the calendar widget.

### How do I delete my account or data?
Please contact us at **jiungdev@gmail.com** and we will process your request.
